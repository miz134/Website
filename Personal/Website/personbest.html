Person Best

Person Best is an Android app created as part of my Software Engineering Course.
On this project, I worked with a group of 6 to create a fitness app.

Used Android Studio, Java, Firebase, Espresso Tests, and automated testing.
Followed Agile development, using Github, Zenhub, and CircleCI.

    Features:
    Connected to google account
    Cloud support
    Begin walks/runs with a click of a button
    Keeps track of past walks/runs
    Keeps track of progress throughout the week and month
    Progress bar chart of your step activity
    Personalized step goals and stride lengths
    Congratulation notifications when you reach a goal or milestone
    Reminds you to go on walks/runs if you haven't met your goal yet
    Add friends and message them
    See friends' activity
